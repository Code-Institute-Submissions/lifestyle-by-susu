mixed okra soup
Makes 3 servings

lin low carb by: Ronke Edoho

  • 6 cups chopped okra
  • 1/2 small onion
  • 2 large bell peppers
  • 1-2 hot peppers
  • 400g shrimps or prawns
  • 2 tablespoons crayfish
  • 1/3 cup shredded stock or smoked fish
  • 2 tablespoon locust beans optional
  • Salt to taste
  • Handful of chopped curry leaves optional

Directions

  1. Blend onion, bell pepper and hot pepper until coarse. set aside.
  2. Place a pot on medium heat, add in crayfish, stockfish, blended pepper, locust beans, salt and 6 cups of water. simmer for 12mins.
  3. Stir in okra and shrimps. taste and adjust seasoning to preference. cook for extra 3min and remove the pan from heat.
  4. It can be served with wheat bread toast
