There are different variations of lunges: the above picture illustrates "walking lunges" Lunges are a great way to strengthen, sculpt, and tone your bodies, while also improving overall fitness and enhancing athletic performance.

Lunges

  1. Stand up straight with your feet shoulder-width apart. Your hands by the side of your body or on your hips.
  2. Step forward with your left leg, putting the weight into your heel.
  3. Bend the left knee, lowering down so that it’s parallel to the floor in a lunge position. Pause for a beat.
  4. Without moving the left leg, move your right foot forward, repeating the same movement on the right leg. Pause as your right leg is parallel to the floor in a lunge position.
  5. Repeat the motion, “walking” forward as you lunge, alternating legs.

benefits of Lunges

  • Weight loss.
  • Balance and stability.
  • Alignment and symmetry.
