everthing salad
Makes 1 servings

For the love of salad by: Susu

ingredients

  • 3 cups greens (kale,spinach, cabbage, lettuce etc)
  • 1 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup cubed watermelon
  • Pinch of cinnamonoptional
  • Lemon juice
  • Feta cheese
  • Olives
  • Olive oil

Directions

  1. Combine lemon juice with salt, pepper and olive oil, set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, toss all salad ingredients with lemon juice mix
  3. Optional: you can add pieces of chicken or seafood to make it a meal
