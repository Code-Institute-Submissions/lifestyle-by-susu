plank
30secs 3sets

Fit fam by: Aziza

The above picture diplays a bodyweight plank, Planks are an ideal exercise for the abdominal muscles exactly because they engage all major core muscle groups. the best part is you only spend about ten minutes max per day in the planking exercise.

Plank

  1. Start the position with your forearms and toes on the floor as seen in the picture.
  2. Keep your torso straight and your core tight and your body in straight line from ears to toes with no bending.
  3. Keep your head relaxed.
  4. Hold this position for 30seconds and repeat the process 3 times.

benefits of Planking

  • Improves core definition and performance.
  • Decreases risk of back and spinal injuries
  • Boosts your overall metabolism
