Beyond body transformation by Zita: 14months and counting!

Meet Zita: she started her fitness journey consistently after a close friend of hers who is a couple years younger suffered a tragic pregnancy and childbirth complications, which was a huge wakeup call for Zita, after which she vowed to herself to make better lifestyle changes get her body stronger and healthier as a precaution for her own pregnancy and child birth journey. To quote her “my goal is to do my best and my part in preparing my body for child birth and being healthy enough to train my children, even though some people will argue that there is nothing you can do about pregnancy complications (pre - eclampsia) , but I believe that if my body is stronger, I can go through pregnancy seamlessly.”

Goals: “To keep on pushing my limits whilst getting stronger and healthier”

Struggles:“Fighting negative energy : mostly people’s perception of physical activities”.

Fitness tip:“Just do it. Start walking, and don't look at anyone or anything, focus on your daily achievements. Stay away from the scale! Celebrate your ability to take 1000 steps, your ability to finish 4litres of water daily, your ability to dress up and show up and do the work required. The exercise that didn't work is the exercise you didn't do. PS: you don't have to join a gym immediately, start with your stairs, your backyard, your couch and a skipping rope. Just start.”

Motivation:“The gains are my motivations: the uplifting moods, the energy burst, the body curves n tones, the strength I have developed, the clear mind, the better immune system etc I can keep going because once you get into the fitness journey the possibilities are endless and every gratifying!”

Meet Aziza and her inspiring journey: 20kg down and still transforming!

Transformation takes time and determination. Aziza surely understands this; she weighed about 80kg three years ago. Aziza was body shamed by people which made her feel insecure in her own body, instead of letting does negative feelings and emotions takeover her beauty and self-worth, she made a choice to join the gym and started making healthier choices. Aziza started with little to no experience in fitness and soon learned her way up, with each milestone she felt better and got stronger. Her initial goal was to join the gym and lose the weight but after she lost the weight, she realized fitness is far beyond what is on the outside to quote her “ For me fitness is therapeutic, I learned the misconceptions regarding working out, so I decided to continue making healthier lifestyle choices”.

Goals: “To maintain my new weight whilst improving my athletic abilities”

Struggles:"fighting the temptations of old eating habits"

Fitness tip: “The fitness world is amdetermination you’re surelyo start working out: try and push your push yourself. Write down your goals and know your mission and always remember fitness is beyond losing the weight.”

Motivation: “It feels good when your body gets stronger. I can do a lot more now and sometimes it amazes me what my body can do, and I also get motivated by my workout partner she pushes me a lot.”
