baked tilapia
Makes 4 servings

lin low carb by: Ronke Edoho

If you don't have an oven, this process can still be achieved in a fry pan. Simply place pepper covered fish in a fry pan and leav it covered on very low heat (no water needed) for 25-30min.

ingredients

  • 4 medium tilapia fish (200g each)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 red chili optional
  • Green pepper, yellow pepper blend it into paste
  • 1 lime

Directions

  1. Wash fish with salt or vinegar, make 3 horizontal incisions on both sides of each fish. pat dry
  2. Season fish with salt and pepper,set aside
  3. Pre heat oven to 400°F (205°C)
  4. Linee a baking pan with foil or parchment, place fish on tray and cover fish with papper paste.
  5. Bake for 25-30min
  6. Squeez lime on freshly baked fish and serve with everthing salad
